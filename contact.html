Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse products on our website, add items to your cart, then click "Order on WhatsApp". Your entire cart list is automatically sent to our WhatsApp number. We confirm and schedule delivery within minutes.
What areas do you deliver to?
We currently deliver to Kanyakumari, Nagercoil, Colachel, Marthandam and surrounding areas within 20km. Contact us on WhatsApp to confirm if we deliver to your location.
What is the delivery charge?
Delivery is ₹40 for orders below ₹499. Orders above ₹499 get FREE delivery! We aim to cover your home within 3–5 hours of order confirmation.
What if I receive something not fresh?
Your satisfaction is our priority! If any product is not fresh or not as expected, simply WhatsApp us a photo and we will replace it at no extra charge or give you a full refund. No questions asked.
Do you accept bulk / hotel orders?
Yes! We serve restaurants, hotels, caterers and corporate clients with special bulk pricing. Contact us on WhatsApp and mention you need a bulk quote — we'll get back to you within the hour.
