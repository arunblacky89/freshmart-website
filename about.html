Our Story

From a Small Stall to Your Digital Supermarket

FreshMart started as a small roadside vegetable stall in Kanyakumari in 2018, run by our founder Rajan with a simple mission — to bring the freshest produce directly from local farmers to families at fair prices.

Over the years, we built trust with thousands of families who relied on our daily freshness. In 2022, we took FreshMart online — making it easy for you to order on WhatsApp and receive the same farm-fresh quality at your door, without the hassle of going to the market.

Today, FreshMart serves 2000+ families across Kanyakumari, Nagercoil and Colachel with 500+ products sourced fresh every single morning.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Freshness First

Every product is sourced fresh daily. We never sell yesterday's stock — if it's not fresh, it doesn't go out.

Fair to Farmers

We partner directly with local farmers and pay them fair prices — cutting out middlemen for better deals for everyone.

Community First

FreshMart is rooted in the Kanyakumari community. We employ locals and support local agriculture.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly packaging, minimal waste, and supporting organic farming to protect our environment.
